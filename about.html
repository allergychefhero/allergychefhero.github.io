About the Game

People with food allergies, particularly children, are a vulnerable group within our population. Young children may be nervous about having an allergic reaction, may be unable to read food labels on their own, and their peers without allergies pose a risk if they are unaware of their food allergies As such, our team created a game for children to learn more about food allergies.
